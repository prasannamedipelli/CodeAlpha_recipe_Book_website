Recipe #3: Omelette

  • Time: Pretty Freaking Long
  • Serves: All the Homies
Ingredients Quantity Price
Mushrooms 1/4 Cup $1.00
Milk 1/8 Cup $5.00
Eggs 2 Eggs $6.00
Spinach 1/2 Cup $15.00
Cheese 1/4 cup $7.00
Tomatoes 1/4 Cup $2.00
  1. Put a small pan on mediuam heat
  2. Add 1 teaspoon of water to a bowl
  3. Add 2 eggs to the bowl
  4. Add any additional spices, mix thoroughly
  5. Add milk, and mix
  6. Pour the mix into the small pan
  7. Add the remaining ingredients while the mixture is wet
  8. Wait until golden brown, from the bottom then flip
  9. Once both sides are golden brown, it is ready to plate and be served!
